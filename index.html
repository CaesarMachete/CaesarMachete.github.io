Arriving in the Yukon

July 15, 2021

I stepped off the boat early Sunday morning. I met my guide, Samuel, at the dock. We procurred a pair of all terrain vehicles and two weeks worth of supplies. We then set off into the wilderness to establish a base camp. Soon I will find out if the legendary creature does indeed inhabit these lands!

First Encounter

July 21, 2021

We made camp at the base of Mount Deathington. The cold set in and we quickly became discouraged after 4 days of no luck. But on the fifth day we heard a noise at night. We shined our flashlights on a shadowy figure as it ran from our camp. The next morning we found a clump of red hair stuck to our perimiter wire! We began to devise a trap to catch the beast!

Caught!!

July 25, 2021

Our plan worked Perfectly! A counter-weighted drop net, baited with a hamshank has ensnared the creature! He struggles mightely as we ponder how we will get him down without hurting the creature or ourselves!
